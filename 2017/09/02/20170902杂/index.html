看博士回忆录，一些感受

Be proactive in talking with professors to find research topics that are mutually interesting, and no matter what, don’t just hole up in isolation.
其实不止在学习中，在工作中也需要多和leader交流，切勿自己一个人在一个小圈子内进行学习工作，因为这样只会事倍功半。古人云三人行必有我师～在交流中可以扩展人的思路从而可以集思广益。

These two opposing experiences taught me the importance of deeply understanding the motivations and incentives of one’s potential collaborators before working with them.
从这两个不同的经验中总结的经验，一个是需要申请终身制的教授、需要发表论文的中期博士，一个是已经是终身教授部急需论文来提升自己地位、和马上毕业在寻找工作的最后一年博士，这里面的发论文动力，规划完全不同……所以在和别人合作前，需要认真揣摩他们的心态，以及潜力，只有看到共通点，合作成功的机会才会大大增加.或许这就是软件中的可行性分析和风险分析。

I tried to “micromanage” myself by setting small, bite-sized goals and attacking them every day, hoping that positive results would eventually come.
设置目标，认清前进方向，要不然真的很容易迷茫

A tenured professor can survive several years’ worth of failures, but a Ph.D. stu- dent’s fledgling career—and psychological health—will likely be ruined by such a chain of disappointments.
踩着巨人的肩膀一步步走向成功，这个之前得有许多默默地作出贡献，而成功却也很少数

Here are twenty of the most memorable lessons that I’ve learned
throughout my Ph.D. years.

  • Results trump intentions
  • Outputs trump inputs
  • Find relevant information
  • Create lucky opportunities
  • Play the game
  • Lead from below
  • Professors are human
  • Be well-liked
  • Pay some dues
  • Reject bad default
  • Know when to quit
  • Recover from failures
  • Ally with insiders
  • Give many talks
  • Sell, sell, sell
  • Generously provide help
  • Ask for help
  • Express true gratitude
  • Ideas beget ideas
  • Grind hard and smart
